# SmartLibrary

Smart Library is an innovative online platform designed to modernize the way you explore, purchase, and read books. It combines the convenience of an e-commerce store with the intelligence of a smart library system, providing a seamless and interactive experience for book lovers.


Features

Browse and Search Books: Easily explore a wide range of books by title, author, genre, or keyword.

Online Shopping: Add books to your cart and purchase them securely through the platform.

Smart Recommendations: Get personalized book recommendations based on your reading history and preferences.

Digital Library: Access purchased e-books instantly online.

User Accounts: Create an account to track purchases, reading history, and wishlists.

Responsive Design: Enjoy a smooth experience on both desktop and mobile devices.
